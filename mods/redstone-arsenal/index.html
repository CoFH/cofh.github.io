Redstone Arsenal is the mod that contains the 'Fluxed' toolset of the Thermal series. It works on its own, but for the best experience, use it together with Thermal Expansion. The mod showcases the implementation of the Redstone Flux API.

Fluxed Equipment
A complete set of RF-powered tools and armor with unique abilities!
Fluxed Blocks
Be hurt and charge your items! What better combination is there?
