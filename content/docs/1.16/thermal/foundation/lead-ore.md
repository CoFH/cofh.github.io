**Lead ore** is a common [ore](https://minecraft.fandom.com/wiki/Ore) that
yields [lead](../lead-ingot/) and small amounts of
[silver](../silver-ingot).

Obtaining
---------

Lead ore spawns between Y levels 0 and 32.
