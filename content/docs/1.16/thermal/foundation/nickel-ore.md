**Nickel ore** is a common [ore](https://minecraft.fandom.com/wiki/Ore) that
yields [nickel](../nickel-ingot/) and small amounts of
[copper](../copper-ingot).

Obtaining
---------

Nickel ore spawns between Y levels 0 and 128.
