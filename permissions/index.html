This page describes what licenses and permissions the different mods of Team CoFH have. Team CoFH has a license of their own called the CoFH Don't Be a Jerk (DBaJ) license, which can be read here.

Thermal Dynamics All Rights Reserved Indubitably

MineFactory Reloaded All Rights Reserved Sure thing
