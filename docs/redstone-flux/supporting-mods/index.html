This is a list of mods that support the Redstone Flux energy system in some way. These are not all of them, but it should at least cover the most important ones.

Mod Description
Applied Energistics 2 A system for storing items and managing automation and item distribution
AutoPackager A machine that crafts items with 2x2 or 3x3 crafting patterns
Big Reactors Multiblock reactors and turbines that generate large amounts of RF
Binnie Mods Addons for Forestry that add more bees and trees, flower breeding, and a genetic manipulation system
Botania Flowers and nature magic. Has a way to convert its own energy to RF
BuildCraft Pipes, fillers, builders, and various other tools for automation
BuildCraft Additions Extending BuildCraft with Fluid Canisters, Kinetic Capsules, Power Tools and more
Compact Machines Adds the ability to build contraptions inside of a single block
Ender IO Compact conduits, painted things and access to your stuff through an eye of ender
EnderTech Ender-themed technological expansion for Thermal Expansion
Extra Utilities Just a bunch of fairly useful things
Forestry Automated farming, bees, trees and more
Mekanism Low, mid, and high tier machinery
MineFactory Reloaded Machines for automating a wide variety of tasks
OpenComputers Modular computers that can be programmed in Lua
Railcraft New rail transport system and various other utilities
Redstone Arsenal RF-powered tools and armor
RFTools Various tools that are related to RF or technology in general
Simply Jetpacks RF-powered jetpacks and flux packs
Thermal Expansion Provides new options for automation and processing
Tinkers' Construct Modular tool and weapon building
