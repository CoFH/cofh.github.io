**Tin ore** is a common [ore](https://minecraft.fandom.com/wiki/Ore) that
yields [tin](../tin-ingot/) and small amounts of
[apatite](../apatite).

Obtaining
---------

Tin ore spawns between Y levels 20 and 60.
