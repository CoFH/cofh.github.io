**Silver ore** is a common [ore](https://minecraft.fandom.com/wiki/Ore) that
yields [silver](../silver-ingot/) and small amounts of
[lead](../lead-ingot).

Obtaining
---------

Silver ore spawns between Y levels 0 and 32.
