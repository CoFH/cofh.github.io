--- title: Thermal Dynamics ---

Thermal Dynamics is the mod in the 'Thermal' series that is all about transport. It contains completely rewritten versions of the well-known Ducts that were once in Thermal Expansion. They were moved to their own mod as a means to organize the content better (and to get Thermal Expansion released within a reasonable timespan).

Ducts
Transport items, fluids and Redstone Flux quickly and efficiently!
Duct Attachments
Control how your ducts work with Servos, Filters and Retrievers, or hide ducts using Covers! (Why would you ever want to hide them? )
