--- title: Phyto-Gro ---

Phyto-Gro is a material added by Thermal Expansion. It is a type of fertilizer that the Phytogenic Insolator relies on to be able to do its work. A variant of the material called Rich Phyto-Gro lets the same Machine work three times as efficiently.

Crafting


(shapeless)
  • 2x Sawdust
  • Niter
  • Slag

(shapeless)
  • Pulverized Charcoal
  • Niter
  • Slag

To craft Rich Phyto-Gro, simply replace the Slag in the recipes with Rich Slag.
