--- title: Rockwool redirect_from: /docs/thermal-expansion/other/rockwool/ ---

Rockwool is a type of block added by Thermal Expansion. It is produced by smelting Slag in a Furnace. Rockwool is nearly identical to Wool from vanilla Minecraft, except that it isn't flammable and is a bit more resistant to explosions.

Crafting

Light Gray Rockwool

Furnace
  • Slag

Rockwool blocks can be dyed by surrounding a Dye item with any color of Rockwool in a crafting grid.
