--- title: Redstone Reception Coil ---

Redstone Reception Coils are crafting materials added by Thermal Expansion. This combination of Gold and Redstone is commonly used when converting Redstone Flux into other kinds of energy, like heat or magnetism.

As a result, Machines or devices that use Redstone Flux usually require Redstone Reception Coils to craft. A mnemonic for this is derived from Gold's chemical abbreviation: Au could be seen as A User.
