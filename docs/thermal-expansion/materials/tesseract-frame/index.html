--- title: Tesseract Frame ---

Tesseract Frames are crafting materials added by Thermal Expansion. They need to be filled with Resonant Ender in a Fluid Transposer before crafting them into Tesseracts.

Crafting

Tesseract Frame (Empty)

Shaped Crafting
  • 4x Enderium Ingot
  • 4x Hardened Glass
  • Diamond
Tesseract Frame (Full)

Fluid Transposer
  • Tesseract Frame (Empty)
  • 1000 mB Resonant Ender
