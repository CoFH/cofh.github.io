--- title: Pneumatic Servo ---

Pneumatic Servos are crafting materials added by Thermal Expansion. They are commonly used to craft devices that do not require Redstone Flux to operate.

This item used to be required to be able to configure the inputs and outputs of Itemducts. However, this is no longer the case; Servos and Filters now serve this purpose.

Crafting


Shaped Crafting
  • 2x Iron Ingot
  • 2x Glass
  • Redstone

If enabled in the config, Pneumatic Servos may also be crafted using Silver, Bronze, Invar or Steel Ingots instead of Iron Ingots.
