--- title: Energy Cell Frames ---

Energy Cell Frames are crafting materials added by Thermal Expansion. They are required to craft Energy Cells. The lower two tiers of Energy Cell Frames can be crafted directly into the respective tiers of Energy Cells. The higher two tiers, however, need to be filled with Destabilized Redstone in a Fluid Transposer before crafting them into the corresponding Energy Cells.

Crafting

Leadstone Energy Cell Frame

Shaped Crafting
  • 4x Lead Ingot
  • 4x Glass
  • Block of Redstone
Hardened Energy Cell Frame

Shaped Crafting
  • Leadstone Energy Cell Frame
  • 4x Invar Ingot

Redstone Energy Cell Frame (Empty)

Shaped Crafting
  • 4x Electrum Ingot
  • 4x Hardened Glass
  • Diamond
Redstone Energy Cell Frame (Full)

Fluid Transposer
  • Redstone Energy Cell Frame (Empty)
  • 4000 mB Destabilized Redstone

Resonant Energy Cell Frame (Empty)

Shaped Crafting
  • Redstone Energy Cell Frame (Empty)
  • 4x Enderium Ingot
Resonant Energy Cell Frame (Full)

Fluid Transposer
  • Resonant Energy Cell Frame (Empty)
  • 4000 mB Destabilized Redstone

Resonant Energy Cell Frame (Full)

Shaped Crafting
  • Redstone Energy Cell Frame (Full)
  • 4x Enderium Ingot
