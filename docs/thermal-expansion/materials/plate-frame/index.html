--- title: Plate Frame ---

Plate Frames are crafting materials added by Thermal Expansion. They are the 'inactive' versions of Plates: they can be placed, but do nothing on their own. They need to be filled with fluids in a Fluid Transposer in order to give them functionality:

  • Destabilized Redstone for Redstone Plates
  • Energized Glowstone for Impulse Plates
  • Resonant Ender for Translocation Plates

Crafting


Shaped Crafting
  • 4x Signalum Ingot
  • 3x Invar Ingot
  • Hardened Glass
