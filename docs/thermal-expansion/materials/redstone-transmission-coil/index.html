--- title: Redstone Transmission Coil ---

Redstone Transmission Coils are crafting materials added by Thermal Expansion. This combination of Silver and Redstone is commonly used when converting various kinds of energy into Redstone Flux.

As a result, Dynamos (which generate Redstone Flux) require Redstone Transmission Coils to craft. A mnemonic for this is derived from Silver's chemical abbreviation: Ag could be seen as A Generator.

Crafting

  • Silver Ingot
  • 2x Redstone
