Dynamo: Fuel Efficiency (seen ingame as the Secondary Gearbox, Flux Linkage Optimization and CryoCoil Regulator) is a type of Augment that causes Dynamos to consume fuel at a slower rate while still outputting the same amount of power, resulting in higher amounts of generated Redstone Flux in the long run.

Levels

This Augment type consists of three Levels. Level I increases the amount of energy generated from a unit of fuel by 10%, Level II by 35% and Level III by 85%.
