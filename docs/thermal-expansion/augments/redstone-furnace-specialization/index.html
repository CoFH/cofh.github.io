--- title: "Redstone Furnace: Specialization" search_headers: - title: 'Trivection Chamber' ---

Redstone Furnace: Specialization (seen ingame as the Trivection Chamber) is a type of Augment that only works on the Redstone Furnace. It limits the Machine to only be able to process food items. However, it doubles the amount of items produced when cooking food and halves the amount of Redstone Flux consumed per operation.
