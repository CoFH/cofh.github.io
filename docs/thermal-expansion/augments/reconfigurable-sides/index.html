--- title: Reconfigurable Sides ---

Reconfigurable Sides (seen ingame as the Integrated Modular Framework) is a type of Augment that allows configuring which sides of a device correspond to which slots or tanks in the device's GUI. It adds the Configuration tab to a device's GUI.

Left-clicking on the sides of a device in this tab cycles through the possible options. Right-clicking cycles in reverse order.

This Augment only works on devices that work with inputs and outputs identified by color-coded openings in their sides. Newly crafted devices that support it come with this Augment already installed, unless disabled in the config.
