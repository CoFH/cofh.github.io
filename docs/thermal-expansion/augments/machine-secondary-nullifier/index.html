--- title: "Machine: Secondary Nullifier" search_headers: - title: 'Nullification Chamber' ---

Machine: Secondary Nullifier (seen ingame as the Nullification Chamber) is a type of Augment that allows Machines with a secondary output slot to continue working when they cannot place their secondary products in that slot. When this happens, these products are simply destroyed.
