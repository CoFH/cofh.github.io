--- title: Lumium Lamp search_headers: - title: 'Radiant Lumium Lamp' ---

The Lumium Lamp is one of the lighting blocks added by Thermal Expansion. The block is functionally equivalent to the Glowstone Illuminator, but it has a different visual style.

The Radiant Lumium Lamp is a variant of the Lumium Lamp that, when on, has a bright halo around itself.
