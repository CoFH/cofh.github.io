Nickel (formerly known as Ferrous Metal) is one of the base metals added by Thermal Foundation. It can be obtained from Nickel Ore and, less commonly, Iron Ore.

Nickel Ore and Iron Ore can also be processed in various ways in an Induction Smelter, with varying byproducts.

Usage

Like most base metals in the mod, Nickel can be used to craft tools and armor. Other than that, it does not serve much purpose, besides being a critical component in creating the much more useful alloy Invar.
