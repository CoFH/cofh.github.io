Platinum (formerly known as Shiny Metal) is one of the base metals added by Thermal Foundation. It is also the rarest of the base metals. It can be obtained from Platinum Ore and, less commonly, Nickel Ore.

Platinum Ore and Nickel Ore can also be processed in various ways in an Induction Smelter, with varying byproducts.

Usage

Like most base metals in the mod, Platinum can be used to craft tools and armor. Other than that, it does not serve much purpose, besides being a critical component in creating the powerful alloy Enderium.
