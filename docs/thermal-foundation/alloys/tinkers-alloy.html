--- title: Tinker's Alloy (Bronze) ---

Tinker's Alloy is an alloy added by Thermal Foundation. It consists of Copper and Tin in a 3:1 ratio.

Usage

Tinker's Alloy can be used to craft tools and armor. It is also an ingredient in various crafting recipes in Thermal Expansion. It is particularly common in the recipes of the mod's Augments.
