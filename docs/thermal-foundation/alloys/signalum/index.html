--- title: Signalum ---

Signalum is an advanced alloy added by Thermal Foundation. It consists of Copper and Silver in a 3:1 ratio, with Destabilized Redstone added to the mix. The alloy is said to be very conductive.

Usage

Signalum is commonly used in devices the functionality of which strongly depends on redstone control. It is also a common ingredient for crafting somewhat advanced devices in Thermal Expansion and Thermal Dynamics. Lastly, it is a critical component for adding locks to machines and various devices in Thermal Expansion.
