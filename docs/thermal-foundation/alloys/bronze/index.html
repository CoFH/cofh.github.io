--- title: Bronze (Tinker's Alloy) ---

Bronze (previously known as Tinker's Alloy) is an alloy added by Thermal Foundation. It consists of Copper and Tin in a 3:1 ratio. Like Copper and Tin, the alloy is quite common in modded Minecraft.

Crafting

Bronze Blend

Shapeless Crafting
  • 3x Pulverized Copper
  • Pulverized Tin

Bronze can also be made using Ingots instead of Pulverized metals in an Induction Smelter.

Usage

Bronze can be used to craft tools and armor. Tools and armor made of Bronze are very similar to their Iron counterparts, except twice as durable. Bronze is also an ingredient in various crafting recipes in Thermal Expansion. It is particularly common in the recipes of the mod's Augments.
