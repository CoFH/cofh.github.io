Axes are tools that are commonly used in Minecraft. They are used to break wooden blocks faster than by hand. Thermal Foundation adds Axe types for all of its base metals and some of its alloys.

Axes can be repaired with an Anvil, using the metals they are crafted with. Axes added by Thermal Foundation also differ slightly from Axes in vanilla Minecraft, in that they can break Melon and Cactus blocks faster than by hand.

Crafting


Shaped Crafting
  • 3x Metal Ingot
  • 2x Stick

Enchantments

Axes can have the following enchantments applied to them:

  • Efficiency
  • Unbreaking
  • Silk Touch*
  • Fortune*
*: An Axe cannot have both Silk Touch and Fortune on it.
