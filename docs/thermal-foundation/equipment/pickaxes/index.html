Pickaxes are some of the most commonly used tools in Minecraft. They are used to break Stone and stone-like blocks as well as ores. Thermal Foundation adds Pickaxe types for all of its base metals and some of its alloys.

Pickaxes can be repaired with an Anvil, using the metals they are crafted with.

Crafting

  • 3x Metal Ingot
  • 2x Stick

Enchantments

Pickaxes can have the following enchantments applied to them:

  • Efficiency
  • Unbreaking
  • Silk Touch*
  • Fortune*
*: A Pickaxe cannot have both Silk Touch and Fortune on it.
