Reinforced Bows are ranged weapons that can be used to fire arrows. Thermal Foundation adds reinforced versions of the Bow from vanilla Minecraft, made with any of its base metals and some of its alloys. Reinforced Bows work nearly the same as their vanilla counterpart, with a few small differences.

Reinforced Bows can be repaired with an Anvil, using the metals they are crafted with.

Crafting


Shaped Crafting
  • 2x Metal Ingot
  • 3x String
  • Stick

Enchantments

Bows can have the following enchantments applied to them:

  • Power
  • Punch
  • Flame
  • Infinity*
  • Unbreaking
  • Multishot**
*: Unlike Bows from vanilla Minecraft, Reinforced Bows do not require a single arrow to be in the player's inventory for this.
**: Does not work properly on Bows from vanilla Minecraft.
