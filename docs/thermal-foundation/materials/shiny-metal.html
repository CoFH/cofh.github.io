--- title: Shiny Metal (Platinum) ---

Shiny Metal is one of the base metals added by Thermal Foundation. It is also the rarest of the base metals.

Usage

Like most base metals in the mod, Shiny Metal can be used to craft tools and armor. Other than that, it does not serve much purpose, besides being a critical component in creating the powerful alloy Enderium.

Naming

The reason the metal is not called Platinum is because it is not part of the seven metals of antiquity. Steve? doesn't know what to call the metal, resulting in the less 'logical' name Shiny Metal.
