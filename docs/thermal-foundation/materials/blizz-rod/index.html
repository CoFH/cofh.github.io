--- title: Blizz Rod ---

Blizz Rods are mob drops added by Thermal Foundation. They are sometimes dropped when a Blizz is killed by a player. Essentially, they are the Blizz equivalent of Blaze Rods.

Usage

Blizz Rods only have a single use: they can be crafted or pulverized into Blizz Powder. This material is a critical ingredient for creating Cryotheum Dust and, ultimately, Gelid Cryotheum.
