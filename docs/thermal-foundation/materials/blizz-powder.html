--- title: Blizz Powder ---

Blizz Powder is a material added by Thermal Foundation. It is created from Blizz Rods. However, it can also be created by infusing Snowballs with Destabilized Redstone using a Fluid Transposer. Essentially, it is the Blizz equivalent of Blaze Powder.

Usage

Blizz Powder only has a single use: it is a critical ingredient for creating Cryotheum Dust, and, ultimately, Gelid Cryotheum.
