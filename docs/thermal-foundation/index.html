--- title: Thermal Foundation redirect_from: /mods/thermal-foundation/ ---

Thermal Foundation is the base mod in the 'Thermal' series. It contains all the basic materials, world generation and mobs that were once in Thermal Expansion. They were moved to their own mod as a means to organize the content better.

Materials
All the basic ingots, alloys, dusts, nuggets, gears and more that are required for the rest of the Thermal series!
World Generation
New ores to mine! Copper, Tin, Lead, Silver and more! Also completely configurable.
Fluids
Unique new fluids with crazy effects when swum in or drunk!
Mobs
Watch out for Blizzes, Blitzes and Basalzes; water, air and earth elementals (and jerks)!
Equipment
Complete sets of tools and armor for almost every metal in the mod!
